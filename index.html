Risk Profiler
Risk Profiler module that provides immediate assessment of a clients’ Risk tolerance. This module is to be used by B2B providers to gauge the ability of a client to introduce various levels of risk into their portfolio. Since Risk Profiling is an integral and formative part of Financial Planning, Finaureus has developed a proprietary Risk Profiling Algorithm that has been extensively back-tested in-order to ensure their risk tolerance limits are in tune with their Demographics, Life Goals, and most importantly, their knowledge of Financial Instruments. The bedrock of a financially successful relationship between the client and the financial provider starts with correct assessment of their Risk Tolerance.
Asset Alocation
Asset Allocation – Financial planning module that allows the clients to set customisable Goal based investing preferences. Based on client inputs, the algorithm will provide optimised allocations which have been derived from time tested portfolio theories. These results would be linked to the clients Risk profile and would enable the client to run various scenarios, while providing real time portfolio feedback to changes in the various input parameters. Our competitive advantage is in not limited to providing a client with the most efficient portfolio based on their asset allocation, but developing products that would help create “Alpha” for clients under different market conditions.
Profolio Creator
Standalone Portfolio creation Module that provides the B2B clients with various proprietary investing instruments that are being developed by our in-house team of Research Analyst along with our Data Science team that combines Machine Learning and AI with predictive intelligence. Portfolio creation will be based on the clients risk taking appetite, their understanding of the risk and reward on each of the asset classes, and their Goal and Investment horizon. Our in-house Algorithm engine will present various what-if scenarios with varying degree of risk that will allow an informed Relationship Manager, or an informed client to select their risk-reward portfolio. Portfolio creator module will also help auto select for clients that are not as informed other categories of clients.
Portfolio Rebalancer
Portfolio rebalancing module which includes the ability to rebalance the client’s portfolio. Rebalancing is a tool that is used to ensure that given market volatility and swings, a client’s portfolio does not get too swayed to a particular asset class, and is in alignment with the original asset allocation that was picked for or selected by the client. The Portfolio Rebalancing Module can be setup to include Fixed Period Rebalancing or Dynamic Rebalancing as per the client’s preference.
Portfolio Tax Loss Harvester
Portfolio Tax Loss Harvester is a standalone module that allows clients of our B2B partners to minimize their tax outflows by either buying or selling and settling out profits / loss between Security & asset class with another. This allows their clients to stay financially prudent with regard to their tax obligations.
Client Onboarding
Standalone Onboarding module which is a paperless solution allowing our B2B clients to effectively onboard their clients efficiently, while maintaining stringent compliance and KYC/AML regulatory checks.
Client Engagement
Standalone Digital Engagement module that provides B2B clients with access to consolidated Analytics, provide content management for Social Media platforms, CRM for Lead generation and conversion tracking.
All-in-One White Label Solution
The All-in-One is a complete solution that will encapsulate all the modules along with execution capabilities, and can be used across the spectrum of clients.
