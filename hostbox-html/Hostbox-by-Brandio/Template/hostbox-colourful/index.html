About Us

o

Finaureus technologies Private Limited has been formed with the objective of building a Robo - Investment advisory platform which combines big data analytics, artificial intelligence and machine learning with deep insights into Investment services to offer disruptive and innovative end-to-end solutions for B2B and B2C segments. The Investment advisory platform offers robo-advisory through automated and low-cost investment and financial advisory services through web and mobile platforms.

We enable our Clients democratize investment advice by creating bespoke digital propositions that provides personalised & timely insights, smart financial planning, automated & personalised investment management solutions at a fraction of the current operational cost.

Our solutions facilitate our partners achieve a high level of efficiency in building long term trust and deepening client engagement, while leaving the core task of providing intelligent and data driven investment solutions to us.

Solutions

Risk Profiler

Risk Profiler module that provides immediate assessment of a clients’ Risk tolerance.

Asset Allocation

Module that allows the clients to set customisable Goal based investing preferences.

Portfolio Creator

Standalone Portfolio creation Module that provides the B2B clients with various proprietary investing instruments.

Portfolio Rebalancer

Portfolio rebalancing module which includes the ability to rebalance the client’s portfolio.

Portfolio Tax Loss Harvester

It is a standalone module that allows clients of our B2B partners to minimize their tax outflows.

Client Onboarding

It is a paperless solution allowing our B2B clients to effectively onboard their clients efficiently.

Client Engagement

It provides B2B clients with access to consolidated Analytics.

All-in-One White Label Solution

It will encapsulate all the modules along with execution capabilities.
