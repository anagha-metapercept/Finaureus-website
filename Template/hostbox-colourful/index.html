About Us

We are a Robo Advisory platform which combines big data analytics, artificial intelligence and machine learning with deep insights into investment services to offer disruptive and innovative end-to-end solutions for B2B and B2C segments.

We offer bespoke digital investment advice across multiple asset classes through our extensive library of proprietary algorithms that create automated, hybrid and customisable investment solutions cutting across all segments at a fraction of the current operating costs.

We enable our partners to focus on building long term trust and deepening client relationships, while leaving the task of providing intelligent, unbiased and data driven investment solutions to us.

Solutions

Risk Profiler

Risk Profiler is a 4-Dimension module that provides a proprietary Risk Score which acts as a critical input for investment planning.

Asset Allocation

Our Asset Allocation algorithms have been built on the foundation of a diverse set of Noble Prize-Winning research which are then further optimised internally.

Portfolio Creator

Scans, filters and selects the underlying securities from a wide universe of investment instruments based on the client's preferences.

Portfolio Rebalancer

Dynamically rebalance the clients’ portfolio periodically to avoid getting swayed from their original asset allocation.

Portfolio Tax-Loss Harvester

Opportunities that allow client to minimise tax outflows and improve returns.

Client Onboarding

Standalone paperless solution allowing B2B clients to effectively onboard their clients efficiently.

Investment Toolbox

Combination of any or multiple of the above modules based on specific client requirements.

Integrated White Label Solution

An end-to-end solution that will encapsulate all the modules for a next generation user experience.

Customised-Automated Digital Platform

A customised platform for any combination of the above modules through our library of APIs.
